About the Convocation

Eastern Africa Statistical Training Center (EASTC) has a great pleasure to invite you in their 7thConvocation ceremony of the year 2024.EASTC continues with the tradition within academic institutions of gathering together academicians,students, alumni,benefactors and industry proffesionals as stakeholders of the university community.


This year's convocation has selected the topic "The emerging data science and digital innovation ecosystem of Tanzania" as the standing point to reflect on the theme of this year's 18 November African Statistics Day. The theme states "Strengthening data systems by modernizing production and use agriculture statistics: informing policies with the view to improve resilience in agriculture, nutrition and food security in Africa"

Agenda

  • 1.Processional: Music and the formal entrance of the academic procession.
  • 2.Opening Remarks: A welcome address by a high-ranking official, such as the university president or dean
  • 3.National Anthem: Performance of the national anthem
  • 4.Invocation: A prayer or inspirational reading
  • 5.Introduction of Dignitaries: Recognition of special guest, faculty and administration
  • 6.Keynote Address: Speech by a distinguished guest
  • 7.Conferring of Degrees: Presentation of degrees to graduate
  • 8.Awards and Honors: Presentation of special awards, honors and recognition of outstanding achievements
  • 9.Student Speaker: Address by a representative of the graduating class
  • 10.Closing Remarks: Final words from a university official
  • 11.Recessial: Music and the formal exit of the academic procession
